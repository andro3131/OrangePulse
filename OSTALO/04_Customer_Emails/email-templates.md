## Notes for Manual Process

**When you receive Stripe payment notification:**

1. Check Stripe dashboard for customer details:
   - Email address
   - Name
   - Plan (Monthly/Lifetime)
   - Payment amount

2. Send welcome email:
   - Use template above
   - Attach OrangePulse_User_Manual.pdf
   - Personalize [Name] and [Plan]

3. Track in spreadsheet:
   - Log email, name, plan, date
   - Mark "Email sent: YES"
   - Mark "TradingView username: PENDING"

4. When customer replies with username:
   - Invite them on TradingView
   - Send follow-up email
   - Update spreadsheet: "Invite sent: YES"

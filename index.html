TrendPullback Strategy

Catch pullbacks in strong trends using dual moving averages and MACD confirmation. Ideal for trending markets with healthy corrections.

Trend MA EMA 200

Confirmation MACD crossover or MA reclaim

Breakout Strategy

Enter on range breakouts with volume confirmation and squeeze filter. Perfect for capturing explosive moves after consolidation.

Best For Scalping, price level targeting

Frequently Asked Questions

The DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) system starts with a Base Order (BO) when your chosen strategy triggers. If the price moves against you, Safety Orders (SO) automatically place additional buys/sells at calculated levels. Each SO is larger than the previous (Volume Scale 1.55x) and placed further away (Step Scale 1.05x). This lowers your average entry price, allowing you to exit at profit even if the initial entry wasn't perfect.

MeanReversion: Best for ranging markets and volatility spikes.
TrendPullback: Ideal for strong trends with healthy pullbacks.
Breakout: Perfect for consolidation breaks and explosive moves.
MicroBreakout: Great for scalping and precise price level entries.

Start by backtesting each on your target asset to see which performs best in current conditions.

MSO (Emergency SO) are 3 additional large orders that activate based on time conditions, not price. They're your safety net for extreme drawdowns:

  • Trigger only after all normal SOs are filled
  • Customizable USD amounts ($10k/$20k/$30k default)
  • Time-based activation (you set future timestamps)
  • Designed for black swan events or extended drawdowns

Most traders set these to dates far in the future and only reduce the timestamp if needed during a position.

The 3-stage Trailing TP system maximizes profits in trending moves:

  1. ARM (Activation): Activates when price reaches your threshold (e.g., +1% profit)
  2. BUFFER: Protects downside - exits if price drops below ARM minus buffer (e.g., +0.8%)
  3. TRAILING: Activates after price rises further - trails behind the peak by your trail % (e.g., -0.5% from highest)

This ensures you lock in minimum profit (buffer zone) while capturing extended moves (trailing zone).
